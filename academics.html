Academics




Mass Academy is a high school for juniors and seniors that is a part of WPI in Worcester, MA. Juniors go to school at 85 Prescott Street, while seniors take college classes at WPI. Each class has about fifty students. During junior year, the class is divided into three sections: Advanced Spanish, French, and Intermediate Spanish. School for juniors go from 7:45 am to 2:45 pm. On most days, there are also extracurriculars that last until 4:30. Juniors take seven classes: Computer Science, Foreign Language, Humanities, Math, Physics, STEM, and STW. Visit the subject pages for more information regarding a specific subject. Visit the Mass Academy website for more information regarding the school. If you have any questions, please feel free to email me at djng@wpi.edu.
